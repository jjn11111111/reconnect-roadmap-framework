## Principles for all phases

1. **Neutrality first** — document failures and Grok's pushback, not only agreement.
2. **No repo merging** — marketing site stays static; apps stay separate.
3. **Falsifiability** — if a claim cannot be tested, label it speculative.
4. **Spoon-feeding no one** — present tools; let the experiencer choose the vortex spin.
