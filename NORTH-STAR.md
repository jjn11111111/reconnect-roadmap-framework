## Not my bag

The slicing, dicing, and hacking that follows publication. I am the **composer**, not the permanent defender of the comments section.

## On stage

- Lead with **felt pattern** and **verifiable math**
- Label **hypothesis** before speculative edges
- Land on **invitation** (middle node, practice), not proof-of-teleportation
- Leave PhDs a claim to check; leave lay people an image they can't unsee

## Off stage

- Framework repo holds depth; talks hold **one idea each**
- Critique layer (`ANALYSIS.md`) exists so I don't have to become it
- Soft shell (transport, institutional narrative) — discuss when asked, don't lead
