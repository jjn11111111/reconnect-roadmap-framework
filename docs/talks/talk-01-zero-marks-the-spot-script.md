# Talk 1 — Zero Marks the Spot
## Full word-for-word script (~18 minutes)

**Title on stage:** *Zero Marks the Spot — Where the Universe Hinges*  
**Series:** re:CONNECT — The Power of −0−  
**Tone:** Wonder first. Wonder without woo. Invitation to see.  
**Stage note:** Open with raindrop if possible — 5 sec loop, then blackout to you.

---

### [0:00 — COLD OPEN]

*(Raindrop on screen. No music. Let it fall once. Blackout. You, center stage.)*

When you were a kid, someone drew you a treasure map.

And at the end of the dotted line — what did they put?

An X.

X marks the spot.

*(Beat.)*

Tonight I want to add one letter.

O marks the spot too.

Same place.

X says *crossroads*. O says *portal*.

Not one instead of the other.

**Both.**

Fully marking the spot.

*(Slide 1: raindrop still — or title slide)*

That's where we begin.

With zero.

And zero is the most misunderstood number on Earth.

---

### [1:30 — TITLE]

*(Slide 2: Zero Marks the Spot)*

I'm Jeremy.

I don't come to sell you a religion or a conspiracy or a gadget.

I come to show you a **hinge**.

A pattern so old it's been hiding in plain sight — in your chemistry textbook, in your rainbow, in the way you were born.

It's called −0−.

And once you see the hinge, the whole world reorganizes.

Not because I said so.

Because your body already knew.

---

### [3:00 — THE GATEWAY YOU'VE ALREADY SEEN]

*(Slide 3: abstract tunnel / circle — tasteful)*

Every one of us arrived here through a circular passage.

The birth canal isn't poetry. It's anatomy.

A tunnel. A funnel. A zero-shaped door.

And when people come close to leaving — in dreams, in near-death experiences, in medicine — they report the same geometries again.

Tunnels. Spirals. Honeycombs. Cobwebs.

Heinrich Klüver catalogued those forms a century ago.

I'm not asking you to believe in an afterlife tonight.

I'm asking you to notice: **the gate is already in the data.**

Round goes in. Round goes out.

Zero isn't the end of the story.

Zero is the **door**.

---

### [5:00 — YOU SIT INSIDE A ZERO]

*(Slide 4: torus wireframe)*

Here's the part science already agrees with.

Everything you can touch is vibrating.

The chair. The floor. The atoms in your left big toe.

Vibration doesn't stay messy forever. It organizes.

And one of the shapes it loves — in fields, in fluids, in the way we model energy — is the torus.

A doughnut.

A zero with a hole through the middle.

*(Gesture: circle in the air.)*

You're not looking at the universe from outside.

You're **inside** the pattern.

Sitting in the hole of the doughnut.

And that hole — listen carefully — is not empty.

It's **full of not-yet**.

Unmanifest potential.

The stuff that hasn't picked a pole yet.

---

### [7:00 — ONE LAW]

*(Slide 5: spectrum bar — − · 0 · +)*

Every spectrum I have ever mapped — and I've mapped a lot — follows one law.

Two poles.

One center.

Left and right are real. They pull. They matter.

But the power isn't in the tug-of-war.

The power is in the **razor** between them.

Not a mushy compromise.

A **hinge**.

The finest edge where both sides are reckoned and neither owns the field.

That's zero.

Not "nothing."

**Neutral form.**

---

### [9:00 — SCIENCE ALREADY DREW YOUR HINGE]

*(Slide 6: pH 7 · green · 0 V — three panels)*

You already trust this idea. You just never named it as one idea.

In chemistry, pH seven is neutral — between acid and base.

In light, green sits at the center of what your eye can see.

In electricity, zero volts is the reference — between plus and minus.

Three different disciplines.

Same architecture.

+ · 0 · −

Nobody owns the center.

That's not mysticism.

That's **measurement theory** wearing work boots.

---

### [11:00 — WHO PICKS THE ZERO?]

*(Slide 7: timeline with BC/AD pin)*

Now here's why this matters for your life.

History uses arbitrary zero points all the time.

Someone says: *everything before this year is "before," everything after is "after."*

BC and AD. Common Era. Fiscal quarters. Your birthday.

The story **reorders** depending on where you plant the flag.

Whoever picks the pivot controls the narrative.

So when I say absolute zero is the center — I mean a center **deeper** than whoever won the last election or the last argument.

A center that doesn't cancel the poles.

A center that lets both be measured **honestly**.

---

### [13:00 — O AND X]

*(Slide 8: O and X on same dot)*

Zero and X.

Circle and cross.

Portal and intersection.

re:CONNECT is spelled with a zero in the middle for a reason.

We are always returning to the hinge.

Reconnecting — not to a tribe, not to a pole — to the **spot both symbols mark**.

---

### [14:30 — THE PULSE]

*(Slide 9: torus ↔ hyperboloid / infinity)*

One more image before we land.

Energy breathes.

Outward — centrifugal — like exhale.

Inward — centripetal — like inhale.

The torus and its mirror, the hourglass shape, oscillate.

That oscillation isn't decoration.

It's the **pulse**.

The fundamental waveform.

You can describe it as high and low pressure, giving and receiving, acid and base — any honest pair of opposites on **one** continuum.

Same law. Different costume.

---

### [16:00 — THE CENTER IS NOT EMPTY]

*(Slide 10: text only — The center is not empty.)*

*(Slow down. Almost quiet.)*

The ancient wisdom associated with zero is mostly lost.

We teach kids that zero means "none."

We use zero and one to build every computer on Earth — and we forget the **spirit** in the symbol.

I'm not asking you to choose.

I'm asking you to **stand at the door** long enough to feel what's stored there.

The center is not empty.

It's the instrument.

The cut so fine it divides without shattering.

The portal where charge crosses intact.

*(Pause three seconds.)*

---

### [17:00 — THESIS]

*(Slide 11: large type)*

**Every spectrum has two poles and a razor at the center.**

**That's −0−.**

---

### [17:30 — CLOSE]

*(Slide 12: embryo silhouette or mirror tease)*

You were born through a zero.

You live inside a zero.

And the spectra you fight in — politics, family, your own head — all of them have a hinge.

Next time we'll ask the personal question:

*What if you're built the same way?*

What if you're not just **on** a spectrum —

What if you're a **mirror** readable forward and back?

Thank you.

*(Walk.)*
