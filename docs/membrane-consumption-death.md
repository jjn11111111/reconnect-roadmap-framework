# Membrane · Consumption · Death

**Thesis:** **To be consumed** and **to die in physical form** are the **same event** in different grammar — locally, the **membrane** releases and **plasma-pattern** disperses from its **dielectric shell**. Eating is death **externalized**; death is consumption **internalized**.

**Status:** Framework cosmology — **hypothesis**. Describes pattern; **does not authorize harm**.

## ⚠ Ethics boundary (read first)

**This notion can justify the most heinous intent if mishandled.** The framework **rejects** that use explicitly.

| Abuse | Why it fails |
|-------|----------------|
| "Victims are only losing local membrane" | **Geometry ≠ permission.** Suffering is real. Consent and dignity matter. |
| Predation as cosmic law | **Half-waveform** — eater without return, honor, or reciprocity |
| Sacrifice ideology / disposal of lives | Map describes **cycle**; does not **rank** souls as fuel |
| Cannibalism, genocide, torture as "release" | **Never** licensed by this cosmology |
| Vampiric extraction (institutional or personal) | Violates **middle node** — pole dominance, not hinge |

**Composer rule (Talk 6):** Lead with **hard core** (verifiable pattern, middle-node ethics). **Soft shell** maps like this are **discuss with labels** — never weapons.

**Jeremy / North Star:** Critique **systems** that normalize extraction; **never** punch down at victims; **never** imply harm is spiritually "fine" because plasma disperses.
